Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before buying. Based on real questions our customers ask.

Are these presets free or do I have to pay? ▼
We offer both free sample presets and premium paid packs. Our free presets let you test the quality before buying. Premium packs range from 600 DZD to 5,500 DZD depending on the bundle size and customization level.
Will these presets work on my iPhone / mobile? ▼
Yes! Every preset pack includes Lightroom Mobile (.dng) files that work perfectly on iPhone and Android. You just need the free Adobe Lightroom Mobile app. We also include a step-by-step video guide for mobile installation.
Can I use these presets for wedding photography? ▼
Absolutely. Our Wedding Essentials Bundle is specifically designed for wedding and engagement photography. The presets are optimized for skin tones, white dresses, and mixed lighting conditions common at weddings. We also offer custom wedding preset creation.
How do I install the presets on Lightroom? ▼
For Desktop: Download the .xmp files, open Lightroom CC, go to the Presets panel, click the + icon, and import. For Mobile: Download the .dng files to your phone, open each one in Lightroom Mobile, create a preset from the settings, and save. We include a detailed PDF and video tutorial with every purchase.
Do you create custom color grading for my specific style? ▼
Yes, this is our specialty! Send us 3-5 sample photos that represent your desired look, and we'll build a custom preset pack tailored to your exact style, camera model, and typical shooting conditions. Turnaround is usually 24-48 hours.
What if the preset doesn't look good on my photo? ▼
Presets are starting points, not magic. Exposure, white balance, and lighting must be close to the preset's target. If you need help adjusting, contact us with your photo and we'll guide you on the best tweaks — or create a custom version for your specific camera settings.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied? ▼
Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all preset packs. If the presets don't match your expectations, message us with your order details and we'll process a full refund. No questions asked.
Do you have Leica-style film look presets? ▼
Yes! Our Leica Film Looks collection emulates the signature muted tones, subtle grain, and timeless aesthetic of Leica cameras. These are premium presets built for photographers who want that classic film feel without buying a $5,000 camera.
How do I purchase from Algeria? What payment methods? ▼
For Algeria: We accept CCP, BaridiMob, and cash on delivery in major cities (Algiers, Oran, Constantine). For international customers: PayPal, Wise, and credit card via secure link. After payment, presets are delivered instantly via email or direct download link.
Can I use these for my car photography page? ▼
Definitely. Our Automotive Cinematic pack is built specifically for car pages and automotive photographers. It enhances gloss, deepens shadows, and adds that cinematic edge that makes cars look expensive and dramatic. Works great for showroom, street, and night shots.
